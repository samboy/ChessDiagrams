  • Internet Explorer 9 and higher (printing doesn't work)

  • Firefox 52 or higher. Probably works with older versions; FF 52 is the last version to run in Windows XP.
  • Current versions of Edge and Chrome.
  • Safari 13.1.3 (MacOS Catalina); note that these chess diagrams do not work in Safari 5.
